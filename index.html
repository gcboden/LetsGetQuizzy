Turn classroom review into a live team game

Bring trivia-night energy to classroom review. Host live, team-based quiz games that reinforce topics, concepts, and learning outcomes.

Great for K–12 classrooms, college courses, Writing 150 sessions, and trainings

Team-based learning (without chaos)

Students collaborate in teams, discuss each question, and choose a Team Captain to submit the final answer—so teamwork feels clear and manageable.

Round-based play for pacing and momentum

Run quizzes in rounds to create natural pauses for discussion, feedback, and leaderboards. It feels like a hosted event, not a worksheet.

Earn Coins & Buy Power-Ups

Students earn coins for correct answers, then spend them on strategic advantages like Extra Time, 50/50, or Safety Net. It adds achievement, leveling-up, and tactical decisions—turning review into something that feels like a video game.

Most quiz tools focus on speed and individual points. Let's Get Quizzy is designed for collaboration, discussion, and instructor-led pacing, making it a strong fit for classrooms that value engagement and active learning.

How it works

Three simple steps to get started

1

Create a quiz from your content

Build around your unit, readings, vocabulary, or learning outcomes (and optionally generate a first draft with AI).

2

Host a live game

Students join with a PIN. Turn on teams if you want collaborative play.

3

Run it like a trivia host

Move through rounds, reveal answers, show leaderboards, and end with a memorable final question.

Perfect for:

• Review days & test prep
• Concept checks & formative assessment
• Vocabulary and content reinforcement
• Writing 150 / research instruction practice
• Staff training & team learning
