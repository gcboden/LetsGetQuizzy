FEATURES

Everything you need to run a great game.

Deep enough for trivia hosts. Simple enough for a Tuesday afternoon class.

AI-powered quiz builder

Generate a complete quiz from a topic in seconds. Edit, reorder, and refine without ever leaving the page.

Team mode with CAOs

Form teams and elect a Chief Answer Officer. Captains lock in answers while teammates strategize.

Confidence wagers

Players bet coins on how sure they are. High risk, high reward — perfect for review sessions.

Power-ups shop

50/50, double points, time freeze, and team boost. Strategy meets trivia for unforgettable matches.

Buzz-in trivia mode

Jeopardy-style. Host reads, players race to buzz in first. Mark answers with a tap.

Live leaderboards

Inter-round standings keep tension high. Celebrate the final results with a burst of confetti.

Flexible pacing

Per-question timers, no-timer mode, or host-controlled. Adapt to classrooms or events.

Join with a PIN

Players hop in by scanning a QR code or entering a PIN. No accounts, no app, no friction.
